OpenCrowdCapture

  • The Map with street level captures
  • Takeout your images from Mapillary to OpenCrowdCapture project

OpenCrowdCapture is a community project to collect street level photo captures.

Servers part of the federation:

  • mapillary-takeout-web.openstreetmap.fr
  • takeitout.cquest.org
